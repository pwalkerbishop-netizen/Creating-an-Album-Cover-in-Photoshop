Introduction

Album cover art is a crucial part of a music release’s identity. In this tutorial, we will learn how to design an album cover using Adobe Photoshop.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Create a New Document

Open Photoshop and create a new document (File → New). Set the canvas to a square aspect ratio suitable for album covers.

Step 2: Import and Adjust Your Photo

Choose a high-quality photo or artwork that will serve as the main visual for the cover.

Step 3: Add Backgrounds or Shapes

Create backgrounds or graphic elements that support your design concept.

Step 4: Apply Creative Effects

Experiment with filters, blending modes, and adjustments to enhance the design.

Step 5: Add Text (Album Title & Artist Name)

Use clear, bold typography to add the essential information.
