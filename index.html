What you'll make

You'll craft a square album cover using Photoshop: set up the canvas, blend imagery with shapes, add effects, and finish with strong, readable typography. This page uses semantic HTML5, includes images & multimedia, and follows Section 508 practices.

Step‑by‑step

1) New document

  • Square canvas (e.g., 3000×3000 px)
  • RGB color; 300 PPI if printing
  • Name your file clearly

2) Imagery

  • Place your primary photo or artwork
  • Use Camera Raw/Adjustments for tone & color
  • Compose with clear focal point

3) Shapes & masks

  • Draw simple geometric accents
  • Create clipping masks for image-in-shape effects
  • Try subtle blend modes

4) Effects

  • Apply tasteful filters (e.g., Oil Paint)
  • Use blur & shadow for depth
  • Unify color with Adjustment Layers

5) Typography

  • Keep to 1–2 typefaces
  • Ensure contrast and legibility
  • Export: JPEG/PNG (sRGB)
